About Us

Crash Consultancy Services was established in 2017 by Graham Fitzpatrick to provide independent, expert motor vehicle crash analysis and assistance with other traffic matters. After serving over 30 years with the Ministry of Transport and NZ Police, Graham is one of the most experienced and longest serving Crash Analyst to ever provide these services in New Zealand. He spent most of this time specialising in traffic-related enforcement and crash investigation, including nine years working as a Crash Analyst in New Zealand’s highest fatal motor vehicle crash district (Waikato), attending and providing in-depth crash analysis investigations and reports.
Graham is recognised in Criminal and Coronial courts as being an expert witness, with his testimony commended by judges and lawyers as reliable and creditable. Now retired from the NZ Police, Graham is offering his expertise to the public.
